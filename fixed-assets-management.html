Overview

Roadmap ERP’s Assets Management module provides a unified system to manage the complete lifecycle of assets — from registration, cost additions, and depreciation to tracking disposals and expirations. It ensures compliance with statutory requirements while giving businesses clarity on Assets utilization, financial impact, and future planning.
