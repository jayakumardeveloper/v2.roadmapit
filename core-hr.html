Overview

The Payroll and HR module in ROADMAP ERP provides a complete solution for managing employee information, attendance, payroll processing, compliance, and reimbursements. From onboarding to final settlement, every HR and payroll activity is streamlined through well-defined workflows and approvals. Integrated with biometric devices, the system ensures accurate attendance capture, eliminates manual errors, and simplifies payroll computation. It covers all aspects of employee lifecycle management including increments, arrears, overtime, travel, loans, tax declarations, bonuses, and full-and-final settlements. Built-in statutory compliance features for TDS, ESI, PF, and Gratuity ensure timely reporting to authorities, while automated pay-slip generation and email delivery save significant administrative effort. With powerful reporting and easy tracking of employee registers, leave balances, salary statements, and loan details, the module ensures both employees and management have transparency, accuracy, and confidence in payroll operations.
