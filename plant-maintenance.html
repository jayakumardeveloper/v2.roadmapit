Overview

ROADMAP Maintenance module helps organizations manage equipment and plant maintenance efficiently. From preventive schedules to breakdown requests and work order tracking, it ensures timely upkeep, reduces downtime, and optimizes equipment utilization while maintaining complete operational visibility.

Key Features

Configuration of Maintenance Tasks and Planners for structured scheduling.

Equipment registration and creation of preventive maintenance schedules.

Creation and approval of Breakdown Maintenance Requests for urgent issues.

Creation and approval of Maintenance Work Orders to track tasks systematically.

Task-wise completion updates for each maintenance work order.

Updating and monitoring of maintenance dues to ensure timely upkeep.

Creation and posting of Equipment Handover records.

Recording and posting of Genset Usage and EB Readings for operational tracking.
