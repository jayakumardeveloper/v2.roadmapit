CRM

Our standalone CRM solution is a robust, independent platform designed to help businesses build, manage, and strengthen customer relationships throughout the entire customer lifecycle — from the first point of contact to long-term loyalty. Unlike a CRM that is part of a larger ERP package, the standalone CRM offers deeper specialization, faster deployment, and greater flexibility to integrate with your existing systems.

Lead & Opportunity Management

Track, nurture, and convert prospects with structured follow-ups, reminders, and automated lead scoring.

Customer Database

Maintain a 360-degree customer profile with purchase history, preferences, interaction logs, and service records.

Sales Pipeline Tracking

Monitor sales performance in real-time with visual pipelines, stage-wise analysis, and conversion metrics.

Marketing Automation

Run targeted campaigns, segment audiences, schedule email/SMS promotions, and measure ROI with built-in analytics.

Quotation & Order Management

Create, send, and track quotations, sales orders, and contracts within the same platform.

Customer Interaction Tracking

Integration Capabilities
