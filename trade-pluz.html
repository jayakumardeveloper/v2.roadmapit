Overview

Roadmap ERP’s Supply Chain (Trade Pluz) module unifies procurement, inventory, sales, finance, and customer engagement into a single streamlined system. From lead management and order processing to vendor collaboration and financial reconciliation, it empowers businesses to operate with agility, accuracy, and transparency. By bridging supply chain execution with customer relationship management, Trade Pluz ensures that every transaction — purchase or sale — is fully traceable, compliant, and profitable.

Key Features

Purchase Order Management

Automate requisitions, approvals, supplier quotes, and PO creation.

Inventory Tracking

Real-time stock updates across warehouses with batch/lot/serial-level tracking.

Reorder & Replenishment

Automated alerts for reordering based on min-max levels and safety stock.

Sales Order Management

Create, amend, and approve sales orders with customer-specific pricing.

Billing & Invoicing

Generate invoices, manage returns, and link sales directly to finance.

Goods Receipt & Returns

Record GRNs, add landed costs, and process purchase returns seamlessly.

Credit & Payment Control

Monitor supplier and customer payments with credit limit enforcement.

Tax & GST Integration

Auto-calculate and post GST and other statutory requirements.

Financial Reconciliation

Sync supply chain activities with accounts payable/receivable instantly.

Reports & Dashboards

Access procurement, sales, and inventory reports with actionable insights.
