Shop Floor

The Roadmap ERP's Shop Floor module forms the center of the entire Manufacturing Execution System, which tracks all manufacturing information in real-time. The module is integrated to the Planning, Inventory, QC, Subcontractor, Gate Entry, and Rework procedures involved in the production process, and effectively monitors and coordinates the entire workflow. It also furnishes you with a visual / on-screen update of all the work-in-progress in one single screen. This helps you acquire complete knowledge of the status of work and respond responsively.

Key Features of Shop Floor

Be facilitated with the module's prompt registry of all aspects of WIP, through all stages, and gain the ability to track down even to the root of all shop floor activities, thereby being able to identify problems quickly and accurately.

The 'Back-flush consumption of materials' facility of the module automatically feeds the production line with materials from Inventory, as per standing instructions, while details of transactions are automatically generated for your perusal.

Never succumb to unexpected inability to accomplish delivery of Finished Goods as the module always keeps you informed of your Inventory levels, so that you never are caught unawares by non-availability of material in Stock.

Be it Repetitive Manufacturing or Discrete Manufacturing or Flow Manufacturing; be all set to cater to the requirements of the workflow through the flexibility offered by the module built to suit any process demand.

The module, in addition to real-time updates, simultaneously analyses the actual state of affairs of the WIP against the Production Plan and prompts you to make changes wherever necessary.

The module's projection of workflow enables you to view the entire process on a single screen with the Roadmap advantage of instant access to work done/ work not done through binary number (0 and 1) representation.
