Shop Floor

The Roadmap ERP's Shop Floor module forms the center of the entire Manufacturing Execution System, which tracks all manufacturing information in real-time. The module is integrated to the Planning, Inventory, QC, Subcontractor, Gate Entry, and Rework procedures involved in the production process, and effectively monitors and coordinates the entire workflow. It also furnishes you with a visual / on-screen update of all the work-in-progress in one single screen. This helps you acquire complete knowledge of the status of work and respond responsively.

Key Features of Shop Floor

Centralized Immediate Insight

Our Shop Floor module serves as a central point for managing production activities with live operational data, providing complete visibility and command across the entire manufacturing floor.

Seamless Workflow Integration

Our system integrates directly with Planning, Inventory, Quality Control (QC), Subcontractor, Gate Entry, and Rework modules to ensure a synchronized and connected production environment.

Streamlined WIP Monitoring

Our platform provides a comprehensive, visual update of all Work-In-Progress (WIP) on a single screen—allowing you to maintain effortless oversight and manage every production stage.

Proactive Decision Support

Our solution empowers your team with instant access to critical production metrics, enabling swift, informed action and immediate adjustments to changes or delays.

Complete Workflow Supervision

Our Shop Floor module effectively supervises every stage of the manufacturing process, ensuring smooth coordination and timely progression through all defined workflows and procedures.

Rapid Operational Awareness

Our system delivers rapid clarity into the status of operations, helping you take decisive action, minimize downtime, and maintain optimal productivity across the shop floor.
