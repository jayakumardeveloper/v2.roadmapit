Overview

The ROADMAP Customer Portal enables seamless interaction with clients, providing order tracking, invoice access, service requests, and feedback mechanisms. It improves customer engagement, reduces response time, and enhances satisfaction across all touchpoints.

Features
Appointment Management Access to Medical Records Billing and Payments Secure Communication Health Insights

Key Features

Customer Profiles

Register, manage, and update customer records securely. This creates a single source of truth for client data.

Order Tracking

Customers can view the latest status of their orders. Real-time tracking helps set the right expectations.

Invoice Access

Download invoices and monitor payments directly. This reduces administrative workload for both sides.

Service Requests

Submit complaints or queries and track progress. Customers gain confidence knowing their issues are monitored.

Feedback System

Customers can log feedback or tickets for follow-up. This ensures continuous improvement and satisfaction.
