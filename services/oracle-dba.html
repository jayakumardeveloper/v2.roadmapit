Oracle DBA

At Roadmap, we recognize that the Oracle Database Administrator (DBA) is no longer just a technical caretaker but a strategic data architect. Traditionally, DBAs were responsible for tasks like installation, patching, monitoring, and backups. With cloud and intelligent automation, routine tasks are seamlessly managed, freeing DBAs to focus on higher-value priorities.

With Roadmap’s approach, DBAs can strengthen data security, optimize system performance, and ensure robust governance across the enterprise. This evolution transforms the DBA role into a powerful business enabler, helping organizations leverage data as a strategic asset for growth, innovation, and long-term success.
