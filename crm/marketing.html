Roadmap CRM Marketing

The Roadmap ERP's Marketing module automates the pre-sales process of planning and executing the conception, pricing, promotion and distribution of your Product and satisfies the objectives of your business, thus, choose your target markets with ease; get, keep, and grow your client- base, through constantly delivering excellent customer value.

Benefits

The automation of functions like identifying prospective customers, trends, track campaigns, maintaining and tracking customer profile, etc.; gives you the potential to efficiently handle marketing activities starting from lead generation up to conversion of order.

The sequential patterning of the modules process helps you to effortlessly register leads which were generated through multiple campaign programs; qualify/disqualify the leads; convert qualified leads into prospect; follow-up prospect; stage visit; and report follow-up/stage visit/visit details.

The module's ability to estimate and project details of budget, expected sales count, and responses, aids you to successfully conduct different kinds of Campaign Marketing, like Trade show, Banner Ads, Conference, Webinar, Cold Calls, etc; and generate new business leads and customer base.

The Marketing module's facility to actively monitor costs of your Marketing processes and project you with accurate real-time costing of your marketing activities, prompt you to take a vector of your expenditure at any time and proceed further based on the cost efficiency.
