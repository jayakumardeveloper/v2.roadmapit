Subcontracting

Fit into your business, the unique added advantage of the Roadmap ERP's Subcontracting module, and almost effortlessly handle all the intricacies involved in your subcontracting procedure, along with getting the liberty to choose between Assembly Subcontracting and Operation Subcontracting as required by the state of affairs of your business. The module automates, tracks, and monitors all third party contract proceedings from the beginning stage of creating Subcontracting Orders till its completion with Invoicing. Stay updated, and have complete access and visibility, of the workflow, as the module systematically channelizes and diligently monitors the entire Subcontracting procedure.

Benefits

Place the Accounted Materials issued to the vendor in the good hands of the Subcontracting modules tracking system, and enjoy the cost-effective benefit of having an Offsite Inventory in your absolute control, even to the extent of tracking the scrap generated during the subcontracted Production process.

The automated processing of the entire workflow gives you the advantage of looking into other affairs of your business that need priority attention and attain maximum time efficiency, while the subcontracting mechanism guided by its impeccable processing capabilities ensures completion of the work.

The fully integrated module ensures eradication of all communication gaps and strengthens the bonding between partners, in further to providing you a centralized control point for managing all the transactions related to the subcontract services, thereby maximizing efficiency of cost and control.

Minimal training required for the easy processing and deploying of the module, gives you the advantage of having to undergo easy training sessions with a short learning curve.

Choose and benefit from the Assembly Subcontracting functionality of the Subcontract module when it comes to the need of contracting a third party for the entire processing of a product from the beginning till its completion.

Choose Operation Subcontracting functionality of the Subcontracting module, and profit from selectively assigning only those processes that need outsourcing, to the vendors, and have semi-finished goods delivered to you for the completion of the product to be done at your premises.
